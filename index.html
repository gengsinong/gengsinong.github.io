Sinong Geng

About

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computuer Science at Princeton University (advisor: Ronnie Sircar). My reserach interests include machine learning, stochastic optimal control and high-dimensional statistics. During my PhD study, I interned at Microsoft Research, Two Sigma, Amazon, and Los Alamos National Lab.

Before Princeton, I got my M.S. degree from the Department of Statistics at University of Wisconsin Madison (advisor: David Page). I received my B.S. degree from the School of Mathematics at Nankai University.
